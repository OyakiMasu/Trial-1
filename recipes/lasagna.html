Lasagna Recipe

The Best Classic Lasagna Recipe - Homemade lasagna full of pasta, ricotta, a homemade meat sauce

Ingredients
  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1/2 lb. sweet Italian sausage
  • 1 medium onion (-chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (-minced)
  • (15 oz.) can tomato sauce

Procedure

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Make a ricotta cheese mixture
  3. Boil the pasta
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Cool
  7. Enjoy
