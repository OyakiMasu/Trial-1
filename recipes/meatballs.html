Old-school Italian Spaghetti and Meatballs

Ah spaghetti and meatballs, an all-time family favourite! Provide your family and friends with an ...

Ingredients
  • 12 Grass-fed beef meatballs
  • 2 tbsp Rice bran oil
  • 1 packet spaghetti or pasta of your choice
  • 4 tbsp Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
Procedure
    Pasta Sauce:
  1. On a medium heat, saut√© the garlic and onion with oil in a frying pan. Ensure that they become soft and almost translucent.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients to your pan and simmer for another 5mins. After this, remove from the heat.
    3. Cooking and assembly:
  3. Again, on a medium heat, use a large frying pan to heat the oil. Fry the meatballs in batches and use tongs to ensure that they are well-browned all over. Once your meatballs are cooked through, remove them from the heat.
  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to the boil. Cook your pasta for 8-10 minutes (or for however long your packet instructs).
  5. During this time, heat up your sauce in a pan.
    6. Serve:
  6. Place your pasta in bowls and top with your meatballs and sauce. Finish with shavings of parmesan.
    7. NOTES TIPS :
  7. Add a little extra to this everyday dish to including some chilli and sundried tomatoes! Make sure you finish with some fresh herbs and extra parmesan for more indulgence!
